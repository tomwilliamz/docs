## Two Paths to a Supercase

When you click **Create New Supercase**, you enter a company domain and Supercase researches the company automatically. From there, you choose your path:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Kickoff" icon="bolt">
    Go straight to a generated business case. Best when you already know the deal well.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Dry Run" icon="clipboard-check">
    Land on the Qualify page first. Fill in discovery details, then generate. Best for building a stronger case.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## The Creation Flow

### 1. Enter the Company Domain

Type the prospect's website domain. Supercase looks up the company via Apollo and pulls:
- Company name, logo, industry
- Employee count and revenue
- Hiring signals and departmental data

If the lookup fails, you can **enter the company name manually** and proceed without research data.

### 2. Set Your Inputs

| Field | What It Does |
|-------|-------------|
| **Addressable Revenue** | The dollar amount at stake — defaults to the company's revenue from research. Adjust to match the actual financial pool for your KPI. |
| **Supporting Documents** | Upload sales decks, call notes, or emails. These give the AI more context for content generation. |
| **Auto-generate Exec Summary** | Check this to have the AI write your CFO one-pager automatically. |
| **Tone Temperature** | 1 (Gentle Persuasive) to 5 (Provocative Challenger). Controls how assertive the generated content reads. |

### 3. Choose Your Path

**Kickoff** — Research runs automatically, then content generation starts. You land on the Executive Summary tab in about 2-3 minutes with a complete first draft.

**Dry Run** — Research runs automatically, but content generation waits. You land on the Qualify page where you can fill in your discovery data (problem, root cause, stakeholders) before triggering generation. This produces a more tailored output.

---

## When to Use Each Path

| Scenario | Recommended Path |
|----------|-----------------|
| You've had deep discovery calls and know the deal cold | **Kickoff** — let the AI draft, then edit |
| You're early in the deal and want to structure your thinking | **Dry Run** — use Qualify to organize what you know |
| You want the AI Coach to score your discovery before generating | **Dry Run** — the coach evaluates each section |
| Quick strawman for an internal meeting | **Kickoff** — speed matters more than precision |
| Building a case to share with a champion | **Dry Run** — the extra qualification step produces better content |

<Tip>You can always go back. Even after a Kickoff, you can visit the Qualify tab, refine your inputs, and click **Regenerate Supercase** to get updated content.</Tip>
