## Share

The **Share** tab renders your full Supercase as a print-ready document — all pages displayed vertically so you can review exactly what the recipient will see.

### PDF Export

Click **Download PDF** to generate a polished document containing:

1. Title page
2. Executive Summary (3-slide deck)
3. Three Options Comparison
4. Problem Analysis
5. DIY Approach
6. Vendor Approach

The PDF is generated server-side for consistent formatting across devices.

### Share via Link

Generate a **public share link** that gives anyone access to the PDF view — no login required. Use this when your champion needs to forward the business case to their buying committee.

<Note>The share link gives read-only access to the rendered business case. Recipients cannot edit the Supercase or see your qualification data, AI Coach scores, or research.</Note>

## Export

The **Export** page gives you additional format options:

### Downloads
- **PDF** — Same polished document as the Share tab
- **PowerPoint** — Slide deck format for presentations

### Copy & Paste
- **Rich Text** — Paste directly into Google Docs, Word, or email with formatting preserved
- **Markdown** — For wikis, Notion, or other markdown-friendly tools
- **HTML** — Raw HTML for embedding

<Tip>The Rich Text copy is the fastest way to get the business case into an email to your champion. Copy, paste, send.</Tip>
