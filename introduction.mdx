## What is Supercase?

Supercase helps sellers build financial business cases based on **actual customer data** - not magic multipliers or arbitrary ROI claims.

Every number traces back to something real: CRM metrics, public financials, or what came out of a discovery call. The result is a business case your champion can defend internally.

## Core Ideas

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="No magic multipliers">
    Most ROI tools apply a hidden "10x improvement" factor. Supercase models the gap between where you are (S0) and where you could be (S1) - a gap that exists regardless of vendor choice.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Three honest options">
    Every business case shows Do Nothing, DIY, and Vendor side by side. We acknowledge that DIY works - just not as well. This builds trust.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Ranges, not point estimates">
    Single numbers feel like sales tricks. Supercase runs 1,000 simulations to show conservative, expected, and optimistic outcomes - the way finance teams actually think.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
