## What Happens Automatically

When you create a Supercase with a company domain, research starts immediately in the background. Two sources run in parallel:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Firmographics (Apollo)" icon="building">
    Company size, industry, revenue, employee count, hiring signals, departmental headcount.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Web Intelligence (Tavily)" icon="globe">
    Strategic priorities, corporate initiatives, challenges, competitive positioning — sourced from the company's website, press releases, earnings calls, and news.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

Research typically completes in **30-60 seconds**. Once both sources finish, an AI-generated **research summary** synthesizes the findings into a single narrative.

---

## The Research Tab

Navigate to the **Research** tab on any Supercase to see what was found. Four sections are available:

### Overview
The AI-generated research summary — a narrative combining all sources. You can regenerate it or edit it manually.

### Company (Firmographics)
Structured company data from Apollo: industry, size, revenue, hiring activity, and departmental breakdown.

### Website (Web Intelligence)
Extracted insights from web research:
- **Strategic priorities** the company has publicly committed to
- **Corporate initiatives** with funding or executive sponsorship
- **Challenges** mentioned in earnings calls, press, or analyst coverage
- **Competitive landscape** and market positioning

### Documents
Any files you uploaded during creation (sales decks, call notes, emails). These supplement the automated research.

## How Research Feeds the Supercase

Research data flows into every part of the generated content:

| Destination | What Research Provides |
|-------------|----------------------|
| **Executive Summary** | Company context, initiative alignment, financial framing |
| **Corporate Initiative** (Qualify) | List of real initiatives to connect your problem to |
| **Problem & Fix** | Industry-specific language and company circumstances |
| **Cost of Inaction** | Financial baseline (M0) derived from revenue data |
| **DIY / Vendor Approaches** | Realistic cost and timeline context |

<Note>Research makes the difference between a generic template and a company-specific business case. The AI uses these findings to tailor every piece of generated content.</Note>

---

## Refreshing Research

Hit **Refresh All** on the Research tab to re-run both sources. This clears the cache and pulls fresh data — useful if the company has had recent news or if the original research was incomplete.

Research results are cached for 60 days. Different Supercases for the same company domain will reuse cached research to save time.
